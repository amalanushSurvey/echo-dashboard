How Invites Are Counted
Per Contact default
Limits how many times a contact receives this survey overall — across all their tickets, orders, or interactions.
Example: Max 2 surveys to John in 30 days, no matter how many tickets he raises.
Per Contact + Variable when enabled
Limits how many times a contact is invited for a specific variable value. The same contact can still be invited for a different variable value.
Example: John won't get more than 1 invite for Ticket #101 — but he can still get one for Ticket #102.
The limit is scoped to the contact + variable pair. Each new variable value resets the count for that contact.
How Rules Are Evaluated

When an invite is triggered, rules are checked in priority order. The first match wins.
