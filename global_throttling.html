How Throttling Works
Global First
Account-level rules run first. Protects customers from multi-team over-surveying.
Survey Override
If a survey or Echo Agent has its own throttling rules enabled, it automatically overrides global limits. No extra setup needed.
When Global Wins
  • Customer fatigue — max surveys across all teams
  • VIP / high-value account protection
  • Compliance & legal limits
  • Cross-channel saturation control
  • Negative experience cooldown
When Survey Wins
  • Post-purchase or support CSAT
  • User-initiated feedback
  • Explicit exceptions (premium users)
